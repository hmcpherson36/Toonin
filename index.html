Beautiful, soulful, wonderful song – but I've come to expect that from Paolo. His other songs like Last Request and Coming Up Easy have been long time favorites but I only just recently stumbled upon this masterpiece.

Another song with a heavenly guitar. The tone is immaculate – as if the melody wasn't fabulous enough. I only just found this song this year but it will most likely be in my top 5 songs on Spotify because of how religiously I play it.
