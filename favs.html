There It Goes - Demo

The Smashing Pumpkins

I love this song to death. Apparently it was a demo so good they couldn't recreate it for the real track, so they just released it as is. I listened to it on repeat my freshman year of college and it was actually one of the first songs I learned on guitar. It's criminally underrated and definitely my favorite of theirs!

Red Hot Chili Peppers

People give RCHP so much hate for their new stuff – and don't get me wrong, some of it is justified – but this song is fan-freaking-tastic. It actually made my top songs on Spotify last year!
